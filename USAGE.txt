 
 Using unbescape from Maven 2
 ----------------------------
 
 
 To use it in your Maven 2 applications, you will need to add it as
 a dependency with:

     <dependency>
       <groupId>org.unbescape</groupId>
       <artifactId>unbescape</artifactId>
       <version>{version}</version>
       <scope>compile</scope>
     </dependency>
